International Student Center

“

The newly established International Student Center located in A113 in 耕莘Building . The ISC here is to support you while you study in FJU. We can assist you from admission, to registration and orientation, housing subsidy and scholarship as well as student activities.

“

The ISC provides advice, information and referrals on matters such as residence regulations, cultural adjustment, volunteer work , overseas travel and study. The ISC staff cares deeply about those who come to campus from other countries, and we are equally invested in domestic students who wish to study abroad on a long term or short term summer program.

“

As part of our overall mission, the ISC advocates for international education as a campus-wide and national priority. Our staff communicates with elected and appointed officials, public and private organizations, and colleagues at other institutions to promote and facilitate international exchange. The culture of the ISC is shaped by a sincere desire to work as partners with all members of the FJU community to help those we are charged to serve.
